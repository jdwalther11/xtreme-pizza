Our Story

John and Zack first met in their 11th grade computer science class, the class bravely commandered by Dr. Saeid Moezzi. After doing many scrums together, they found they both had a profound love for pizza. It was at this moment when XTREME pizza was born. After leaving Sage Creek High School, they saved up some money by selling homemade pizzas on the street corner. When they had saved up enough dough, they opened their first XTREME pizza restraunt, and the rest is history!
